Starting a workout routine doesn’t need to be complicated.

### Warm-up (3 minutes)
- Arm circles  
- Light jogging or marching  

### Main workout (15 minutes)
- 10 squats  
- 10 push-ups (knees okay!)  
- 20 jumping jacks  
- 10 lunges per leg  
Repeat 2–3 times.

### Cooldown (2 minutes)
Gentle stretching, focusing on legs and shoulders.
